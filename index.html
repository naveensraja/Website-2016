Hey there, I'm Naveen. I'm trained as a researcher, I think like an entrepeneur & I communicate like a designer.

Story telling is one of the world’s oldest art forms. Much like how no two days are the exact same, my story is different than most. It's roots are in laughter, great conversation with folk from all over the world and one motorcycle accident in Indonesia. I have embraced challenge and converted it into beautiful moments of clarity and break-through. This website is how I as a passionate designer want to share my story. Work represents where I spend my time. It is a weird intersection of enterprise software, facilitation, entrepreneurship and traveling. I love it. Blending the lines of what design is and what it could be, is where I find inspiration.

I am trained as a researcher.

I approach every problem with research and utilize what my end users want to fuel my decisions.

I think like an entrepeneur.

TravelMore KickStarter

TravelMore is an eCommerce company, which focuses on sourcing and selling travel gear. In the summer of 2016, they were starting to encounter a growth problem. Their co-founder reached out to me to consult with the team as a design strategist. Over the course of two months, I was able to build an understanding of the user, establish a growth strategy, build a full kickstarter campaign and bring in ~900 sales equaling $50,000. IBM Security Design Practices

I joined IBM Security to be their portfolio level researcher. Two weeks in, I took the role to vision and build a design practices program. I applied my entrepreneurial mindset to expand design thinking at a rapid pace. In six months, I have helped many product teams become user centered by using workshops as a tool. The program has been a contributor to over seven million dollars in sales and helped save millions in development costs. Click here to read more...
